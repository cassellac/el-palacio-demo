Menu Our Story

Welcome to El Palacio

For generations, our doors have been open to travelers, families, and friends seeking a taste of authentic Mexican hospitality. Nestled within the historic 1888 Luthy Block on legendary Route 66, El Palacio is more than a restaurant—it's a gathering place where warm meals and warmer welcomes create memories that last a lifetime. Pull up a chair, you're family here.
